Yorkies

A persone who loves Nature and It's creations



From the taste of hometown to a heartwarming homemade brand in Pattaya

Founded in 1999 by an English couple, Norman and Eileen Denning, Yorkies was born out of a love for tradional British food and a desire to share the flavors of home with the local community. What started as a response to homesickness quickly became a beloved destination for both expatriates and THai locals seeking high-quality, classic English fare. Yorkies began by making homemade pies, bacon and sausages-with care and attention in every step of the process. We use only premium ingredients, bake fresh daily, and most importantly, we never use preservatives. The freshness, purity, and heartiness can be tasted in every bite. From a small shop near Jomtien Beach with just a handful of menu items. Yorkies grew steadily through its commitment to authentic taste and quality. Over time, it earned a loyal customer base, particularly among foreigners living in or visiting Pattaya. Today, Yorkies have expanded its production and now distributes products to Big C and Villa Market branches in both Pattaya and Bangkok. Whether you're missing the taste of home or simply looking ofr delicious, homemade food, our products are now more accessible than ever. For over two decades, Yorkies has been more than just a restaurant-it represents warhth, tradition, and the comforting spirit of home-cooked meals passed down through generations, all delivered with the same passion and dedication that started it all.
